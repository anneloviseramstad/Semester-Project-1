Energetica Exhibition on Loan from NEMO Science Museum

We are excited to announce the arrival of the Energetica exhibition at the Community Science Museum, on loan from the renowned NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam.

About the Energetica Exhibition

The Energetica exhibition provides a unique opportunity to explore the incredible power of natural elements and the innovative technologies developed to harness them.

This interactive journey through energy conversion showcases how renewable sources are transforming our approach to power generation and environmental conservation.

INTERACTIVE AREA

DATE: Friday 14/6

LOCATION: First Floor, West Wing

Featured Installations

  • Solar Power Illumination: Experience how solar panels absorb sunlight and convert it into electricity through interactive displays that illuminate the exhibition space. Learn about the mechanics and benefits of photovoltaic cells in real-time.
  • Wind Island: Step onto 'Wind Island' where you can interact with actual wind turbines. This installation not only demonstrates how turbines capture wind energy but also allows you to control the turbines to understand the principles of wind power generation.
  • Hydrodynamic Hub: Explore the power of water in generating electricity through our hydrodynamic models. Visitors can manipulate water flow to simulate the generation of hydroelectric power, observing the potential of hydropower in sustainable energy development.
