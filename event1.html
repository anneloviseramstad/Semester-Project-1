Visiting Professor of Aeronautics: Professor Sheila Widnall

We are thrilled to welcome Professor Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology to our institution as a visiting professor of aeronautics.

About Professor Sheila Widnall

Professor Sheila Widnallâ€™s career is distinguished by her contributions to the fields of aeronautics and astronautics. As one of the leading experts in fluid dynamics, her research has significantly advanced our understanding of the behavior of aircraft and spacecraft in flight.

Her dedication to science is also marked by her historical achievement as the first woman to lead a branch of the U.S. military, serving as Secretary of the Air Force, where she oversaw technological advancements and strategic initiatives that have shaped modern aerospace endeavors.

During her tenure with us, Professor Widnall will deliver a series of three lectures focused on the past, present, and thrilling future of aeronautics.
