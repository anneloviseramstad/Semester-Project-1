Night in the Museum: A Family Sleepover Adventure

Join us for an unforgettable Night in the Museum! Set up camp beside awe-inspiring dinosaurs and engaging science exhibits. Bring your sleeping bags and prepare for a night of adventure!

A Night to Remember

Join us for an extraordinary Night in the Museum, a special event designed for families to experience the magic of science and history after dark.

This unique sleepover at the museum offers a rare opportunity to spend an evening surrounded by the wonders of our extensive exhibits, from towering dinosaur skeletons to intriguing science displays.

EVOLUTION AREA

DATE: Friday 25/5

LOCATION: Second Floor, East Wing

Sleepover Information:

  • The evening will kick off with a guided tour highlighting fascinating facts about the creatures that once roamed the Earth and the latest scientific discoveries that have captivated the scientific community.
  • As the night deepens, engage in hands-on activities and workshops designed to educate and entertain participants of all ages. Discover the principles of paleontology by examining real fossils, or learn about the stars as you stargaze from the comfort of our atrium.
  • After a series of interactive sessions, gather around for storytelling under the glow of the dinosaur exhibits, where tales of ancient times and mythical creatures come to life, thrilling and inspiring the young minds around.
